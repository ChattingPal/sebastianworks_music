About Me

pssst!! go to the bottom of the page to see subscribers!


Sebastian Berg is a young boy, born in 2010, 2nd of August. Sebastian started his career back in 2020-21, with some pop/indie pop. He kept making music and uploaded to SoundCloud until he decided, it was time to start the serious work. Then he began with his first release, Sleeping Angel.

Sebastian got his inspiration from; Gorillaz, Joost Klein and Melanie Martinez. He currently is inspired by a small indie band, Hey Julis! It was them who inspired Sebastian to make this website!
